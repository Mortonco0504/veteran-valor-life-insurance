Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Veteran Valor different from other life insurance companies?

Veteran Valor specializes exclusively in life insurance for veterans, military personnel, and first responders. We offer exclusive discounts, no-medical-exam options for qualified veterans, and a dedicated support team of veterans who understand your unique needs.

Do I need a medical exam to get coverage?

Many qualified veterans can get coverage without a medical exam through our simplified issue policies. However, some policies may require a medical exam depending on your age, coverage amount, and health status.

How much can I save compared to standard life insurance?

Veterans can save up to 40% on life insurance premiums compared to standard policies. The exact savings depend on your age, health, coverage amount, and military service.

What types of military service qualify for veteran discounts?

We offer discounts for all branches of military service including Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, Coast Guard, National Guard, and Reserves. We also cover first responders including police, firefighters, and paramedics.

How quickly can I get coverage?

Many veterans can get instant approval and coverage activation within 24-48 hours. The process is streamlined and designed specifically for military personnel and veterans.
