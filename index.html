Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a medical exam to get life insurance?

No medical exam required for most policies! We offer several no-exam life insurance options specifically designed for veterans:

  • Simplified Issue: No medical exam, just health questions
  • Guaranteed Issue: No medical exam or health questions
  • Accelerated Underwriting: Quick approval using existing medical records

While policies with medical exams often offer lower rates, our no-exam options provide immediate coverage starting at $30/month for term life and $50/month for whole life.

How much life insurance coverage do I need?

Coverage amounts typically range from $10,000 to $2,000,000. Here's how to calculate your needs:

  • Income Replacement: 10-12 times your annual income
  • Debt Coverage: Mortgage, car loans, credit cards
  • Education Costs: College tuition for children
  • Final Expenses: Funeral costs and medical bills
  • VA Benefits: Consider your existing VA life insurance

Veteran-Specific Considerations: Factor in your military pension, VA disability benefits, and any existing SGLI coverage. Our specialists can help you determine the right amount for your unique situation.

Are there special rates for veterans?

Yes! Veterans receive exclusive discounts and special programs.

  • Veteran Discounts: Up to 15% off standard rates
  • Military Service Recognition: Special underwriting consideration
  • VA Benefits Integration: Coordinate with your existing VA coverage
  • Service-Connected Disability: Specialized programs available
  • Combat Veteran Programs: Enhanced benefits for combat service

Our Pricing: Term life starting at $30/month, whole life at $50/month, and universal life at $100/month. These rates are specifically designed for veterans and their families.

Can I get life insurance if I have a service-connected disability?

Absolutely! Veterans with service-connected disabilities can qualify for life insurance.

  • Guaranteed Issue: Available regardless of health conditions
  • Simplified Issue: No medical exam, limited health questions
  • VA Disability Rating: Special consideration for VA-rated conditions
  • Combat-Related: Enhanced programs for combat-related disabilities
  • Mental Health: Specialized programs for PTSD and other conditions

Important: Your VA disability rating and the specific nature of your service-connected condition will determine the best available options. Our specialists are trained to work with veterans with disabilities and can help you find the right coverage.

How long does it take to get approved?

Approval times vary by policy type:

  • Instant Approval: Some no-exam policies approved same day
  • 24-48 Hours: Most simplified issue policies
  • 1-2 Weeks: Accelerated underwriting with medical records
  • 2-6 Weeks: Traditional policies with medical exams
  • Same Day Coverage: Immediate temporary coverage available

Veteran Advantage: Our streamlined process for veterans often results in faster approvals. We can also provide temporary coverage while your application is being processed.
